Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) section! We understand that navigating the complexities of cybersecurity, especially offensive security, can raise many questions. Our goal here is to provide clear, concise answers to the most common inquiries we receive about our services, methodologies, and what you can expect when partnering with Metaspoilt Security.

We've compiled this resource to help you quickly find the information you need, whether you're curious about our penetration testing process, the scope of our red team engagements, or the benefits of a comprehensive vulnerability assessment. Our aim is to demystify offensive security and ensure you feel confident and informed.

If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out directly. Our team of experts is always ready to provide personalized insights and discuss how our tailored cybersecurity solutions can meet your specific needs.

Do You Take Jobs Out Of Cameroon?

Yes, absolutely. Metaspoilt Security proudly offers our full suite of offensive cybersecurity services to clients located anywhere in the world. Our expertise and capabilities are not confined by geographical borders; we are equipped to serve organizations globally, providing the same high standard of penetration testing, red teaming, and vulnerability assessment services regardless of your operational base.

We leverage secure remote capabilities and adhere to established international protocols to ensure seamless and effective engagements with clients across different continents. Our commitment is to help organizations worldwide strengthen their defenses, making top-tier cybersecurity accessible and effective for all. Depending on your specific needs, we are open to travelling as well.

How Is Payment Processed?

Payment for Metaspoilt Security's services is typically processed via bank transfer (wire transfer) to ensure secure and trackable transactions for both domestic and international clients. Upon agreement of the scope of work, we will issue an invoice detailing the services rendered and the total cost. This invoice will include all necessary banking details, such as our account number, swift code (for international transfers), and bank name, to facilitate a smooth and direct transfer of funds.

For larger projects or specific client needs, we may also discuss milestone-based payments, where a percentage is paid upfront to initiate the project, and subsequent payments are released upon the completion of agreed-upon project phases. We are committed to transparency in all our financial dealings and are happy to discuss any specific payment terms or requirements you may have to ensure a mutually convenient arrangement.

Where Are you Located

Metaspoilt Security is strategically located in the vibrant economic hub of Douala, Cameroon. Our physical office is situated within Immeuble Charles de Gaulle, Bonajo. This central location allows us to effectively serve our local clients while also providing a solid operational base for our global engagements.

While our physical presence is in Douala, our operational model is built to be highly flexible and globally responsive. We leverage secure, remote collaboration tools and methodologies, enabling us to deliver our advanced offensive cybersecurity services to organizations across different continents with the same efficiency and dedication as we do for our local partners.
